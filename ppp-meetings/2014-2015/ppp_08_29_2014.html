PPP

The Plaid Parliament of Pwning is CMU's computer security competition team! We play in and run security competitions, and many people here do (or have done) research in security. Anyone (from CMU) is welcome to join the club and play with us!

Computer Security Competitions

  • CCDC (we don't play)
  • Capture the Flag (we do play)

Capture the Flag

  • Attack/Defense: everyone has a server, you own other teams while trying not to get owned
  • Jeopardy: list of challenges to solve, race to the most points

Why join PPP/play CTFs?

  • They're fun!
  • We're pretty friendly
  • You learn a lot
  • Everyone is welcome
